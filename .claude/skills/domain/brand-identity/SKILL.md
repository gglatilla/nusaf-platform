# Nusaf Brand Identity

## Overview

This skill defines the visual identity and brand guidelines for Nusaf Dynamic Technologies. All user-facing interfaces — website, customer portal, internal tools, documents, and marketing materials — must adhere to these standards.

## Brand Essence

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Company Name** | Nusaf |
| **Full Legal Name** | Nusaf Dynamic Technologies |
| **Tagline** | Driving Dynamic Solutions |
| **Industry** | Industrial B2B — Conveyor components, power transmission |
| **Personality** | Premium, modern, professional, trustworthy, technical |
| **Location** | South Africa (Johannesburg HQ, Cape Town, Mbombela) |

## Logo Usage

**Primary:** "Nusaf" wordmark in Inter Bold, #0F172A on light backgrounds
**On Dark:** "Nusaf" wordmark in Inter Bold, #FFFFFF on dark backgrounds
**Minimum Size:** 80px width for digital, 20mm for print
**Clear Space:** Minimum padding equal to the height of the "N"

**Do:**
- Use the wordmark consistently
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use approved colour combinations

**Don't:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Change the typeface

## Motion & Animation

**Principles:**
- Subtle and purposeful — animations should inform, not distract
- Fast and responsive — users shouldn't wait for animations
- Consistent timing across the application

**Timing:**
| Type | Duration | Easing |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Micro** | 100-150ms | `ease-out` |
| **Standard** | 200-300ms | `ease-in-out` |
| **Complex** | 300-500ms | `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` |

**Common Animations:**
- Button hover: Scale 1.02, shadow increase, 150ms
- Card hover: translateY(-2px), shadow increase, 200ms
- Modal enter: Fade in + scale from 0.95, 200ms
- Dropdown: Fade in + translateY(-4px to 0), 150ms
- Page transitions: Fade, 200ms

## Photography & Imagery

**Style:**
- High-quality industrial photography
- Clean, well-lit environments
- Focus on machinery, precision, and craftsmanship
- Authentic working environments (not overly staged)

**Treatment:**
- Full colour, no filters
- High contrast for clarity
- Avoid heavy post-processing

**Subjects:**
- Conveyor systems in operation
- Close-ups of chains, sprockets, components
- Manufacturing facilities
- Engineering/technical processes

---

## Voice & Tone

**Voice Attributes:**
- **Professional** — Expert knowledge, industry authority
- **Clear** — No jargon without explanation, direct communication
- **Helpful** — Solution-oriented, customer-focused
- **Confident** — Assured quality, reliable partner

**Writing Guidelines:**
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use active voice
- Be specific with numbers and data
- Avoid superlatives without proof ("best", "leading")
- Technical accuracy is paramount

**Example:**
❌ "We offer the best conveyor solutions in the industry"
✅ "Modular chain systems configured to your specifications, delivered within 5 days"

## Application Guidelines

### Website
- Light theme only
- Hero sections may use subtle gradients (white to snow)
- Electric Blue for all CTAs
- Dark footer using Ink #0F172A

### Customer Portal (Web App)
- Light theme for main content area
- Dark sidebar using Ink #0F172A for navigation
- White cards on Snow #F8FAFC backgrounds
- Electric Blue for primary actions

### Documents (Quotes, Invoices, PDFs)
- White background
- Ink for headings
- Electric Blue for accents (headers, totals)
- Professional, clean layout with ample whitespace

### Email Communications
- White background
- Minimal design, text-focused
- Electric Blue for buttons/links
- Nusaf wordmark in header
